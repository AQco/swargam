About Us

Siraj Associates is a construction and contracting firm based in Kochi offering services in civil construction, technical consultancy, project management, and interior and landscaping works. The firm was established by Mr. Mohammed Siraj, a chartered civil engineer with more than 30 years of experience in civil contracting and construction management.

Our experience and expertise lie in civil and finishing works of high-rise buildings, commercial and institutional structures, and residential villas with properties panned in Kerala and Bangalore.

Backed up by an adept team of professionals and headed by Mr. Siraj, we strive to offer services with an uncompromised commitment to Quality, Health, and Safety.
